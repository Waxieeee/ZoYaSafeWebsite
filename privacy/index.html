Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24 May 2026  ·  Last updated: 24 May 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Plain-language summary ZoyaSafe does not collect personal information from children. We do not run ads. We do not sell data. The app is designed to be a closed, safe environment — what your child watches stays on the device and is never shared with third parties.

1. Who we are

ZoyaSafe is a curated video app for children, built and operated as an independent product. The app is available on Android via the Google Play Store. Our registered support contact is support@zoyasafe.com.

For the purposes of Indian data protection law (the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023) and international standards including the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), ZoyaSafe acts as the data fiduciary / operator for the limited technical data described in this policy.

2. Who this app is designed for

ZoyaSafe is designed for use by children under the supervision of a parent or guardian. Children do not create accounts, enter personal information, or interact with other users. The parent or guardian manages the subscription and app settings; the child only interacts with the video-viewing interface.

3. Data we collect — no personally identifiable information, from anyone

ZoyaSafe collects no personally identifiable information (PII) from any user — neither the child nor the parent or guardian. The only data we handle is anonymous and technical in nature, detailed below.

Data type Collected? Purpose
Child's name No Not requested or stored
Child's email or contact info No Not requested or stored
Parent / guardian name or email No No account creation required
Location data No Not accessed
Camera / microphone No Not accessed
Viewing history No Not logged or transmitted
Android Device ID Yes Required by Google Play to verify subscription entitlement via RevenueCat. Not linked to a child profile. See §4.
Subscription status Yes To determine whether an active subscription exists. Managed by RevenueCat. See §5.
Crash / error logs Yes Anonymous technical diagnostics only. No user-identifiable information.

4. Android Device ID

The app uses a device-level identifier (Android ID) solely to verify that a valid subscription exists on the device. This identifier is:

  • Not linked to the child's identity or any personal profile
  • Not shared with advertising networks or analytics providers
  • Not stored on our servers beyond what is necessary for entitlement verification
  • Processed in accordance with Google Play's developer terms

5. Subscriptions and payments (RevenueCat)

ZoyaSafe uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to payment card details.

RevenueCat receives your device's anonymous purchase token from Google Play to verify subscription status. RevenueCat's own privacy policy governs their data handling: revenuecat.com/privacy.

New subscribers receive a 7-day free trial, enforced by ZoyaSafe's backend systems. After the trial period, a subscription is required to continue using the app: either a monthly plan (₹349/month) or an annual plan (₹3,499/year). Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time through the Google Play account settings on your device.

6. Children's privacy (COPPA & DPDP Act)

We take children's privacy seriously.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or under 18 as defined by applicable Indian law).
  • The app does not contain social features, in-app messaging, user-generated content, or behavioural advertising.
  • No child-facing screen requests or displays personal information.
  • All content visible in the app is pre-curated by us; children cannot browse open internet content.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal data relating to a child, please contact us immediately at support@zoyasafe.com and we will delete it promptly.

7. YouTube content and the IFrame player

ZoyaSafe uses the YouTube Data API to source curated video content. Videos play via YouTube's privacy-enhanced embed (youtube-nocookie.com) loaded inside the app. The video player is operated by Google, not ZoyaSafe. Google may set cookies or collect technical data through this embed in accordance with their own privacy policy. We use YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode specifically to reduce tracking.

The app intercepts all navigation within the player to prevent children from following related video links, comments, or any path outside the curated content. Children cannot reach open YouTube through the app.

Our use of the YouTube API is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and YouTube's Terms of Service.

We do not integrate any advertising SDKs, social login SDKs, or third-party analytics beyond the anonymous crash reporting described in §3.

8. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade any data. We do not share data with third parties except as described in this policy (subscription verification via RevenueCat, and YouTube API usage via Google). We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of users, but we have no commercial data-sharing arrangements.

9. Data retention

Subscription entitlement data is retained only for as long as your subscription is active. Anonymous crash logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days. We do not maintain persistent user records beyond what is necessary for the app to function.

10. Security

The app communicates exclusively over HTTPS. Our backend infrastructure is hosted on Railway, with the database on Supabase, both of which maintain industry-standard security practices. We do not store sensitive personal or payment data on our own servers.

11. Your rights

As a parent or guardian, you may at any time:

  • Request confirmation of what (if any) data we hold related to your device or subscription
  • Request deletion of any data we hold
  • Cancel your subscription through Google Play

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@zoyasafe.com. We will respond within 30 days.

12. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify subscribers via the email associated with their Google Play account. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
