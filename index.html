The Problem

YouTube wasn't built
for five-year-olds.

YouTube's algorithm is optimised for engagement - not your child's wellbeing. Every "Up Next" is a door into the unknown.

🕳️

Algorithm Rabbit Holes

One animated video leads to ten more, each increasingly random and un-vetted. YouTube's engine prioritises watch time - not child safety.

😨

Inappropriate Suggestions

Related videos, comments, and YouTube's homepage can surface disturbing content even on family-friendly searches. No filter is foolproof.

📵

No Real Parental Control

YouTube Kids is better than nothing - but it's still algorithm-driven. You can't choose specific creators your child watches, only categories.

How It Works

Simple by design.

Three steps. Total control. No technical setup required.

1

You Build the Library

Search for YouTube channels directly in the app and add them to your approved list. Only channels you've whitelisted will ever appear for your child.

2

Kids Watch Freely

Your child taps their profile and sees only the channels you've approved - no login, no passwords, no distractions. Just their favourite creators, safe and locked in.

3

Zero Escape Routes

Kiosk mode locks the app to the screen. The YouTube player blocks all navigation outside approved content. Parent exit requires a math quiz - kids can't trick their way out.

Features

Every detail built for peace of mind.

We've thought through every escape route a child might find - and closed it.

Core

Whitelist-Only Channels

If you haven't added it, it doesn't exist. No browsing, no discovery outside your approved list.

Security

Kiosk Mode

Android's built-in lock task mode pins the app to the screen. No back button, no home button, no recent apps.

Security

MathGate Parent Auth

A simple math quiz blocks any attempt to exit the app or access parent settings. No PINs to share, no way to brute-force.

Privacy

No Ad Networks

We have no advertising SDK and earn nothing from ads. Some YouTube videos may show their creator's own pre-roll - that's the creator's setting, outside our control.

Content

Shorts & Livestream Filter

YouTube Shorts and live streams are automatically filtered out. Only proper, curated videos appear in the feed.

Family

Multiple Kid Profiles

Each child gets their own profile with their own channel whitelist. A 3-year-old and a 9-year-old can share one device safely.

Player

Blocked Navigation

Every exit route inside the YouTube player - related videos, channel links, descriptions - is intercepted before your child can follow it.
